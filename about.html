About Us

Who we are ?

With over several years of involvement with Plywood seller and assembling architect plywood, we keep the nature of Advance embellishing covers on main concern. For this, Luan Plywood has recruited a group of value checkers who complete various quality checks of the items right from buying natural substances, and assembling cycles to the last conveyance.

Our group tests items on severe quality estimates to ensure they can endure changing temperature conditions. Development and abilities are the central point behind the achievement and Luan Plywood focus on the expertise improvement of our work force. Considering that, we can make our assembling processes more flexible, less energy-consuming, and all the more harmless to the ecosystem.
